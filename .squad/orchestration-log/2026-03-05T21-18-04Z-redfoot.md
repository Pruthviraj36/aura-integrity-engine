## Validation Criteria

**WCAG AA Standards:**
- Body text (normal weight): minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- Large text (18px+ or 14px+ bold): minimum 3.0:1 contrast ratio
- UI components: minimum 3.0:1 contrast ratio

**Test Backgrounds:**
- Light: hsl(210 40% 98%) — light gray (approx. card background)
- Dark: hsl(222 47% 4%) — deep navy (approx. page background)
